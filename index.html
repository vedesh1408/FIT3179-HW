About the Report

The World Happiness Report is an annual publication that assesses the state of global happiness and well-being. It takes into account various factors, including economic indicators, social support, life expectancy, and more, to rank countries based on their happiness levels.

Key Findings

Some of the key findings from the report include:

  • The happiest countries tend to have higher GDP per capita.
  • Social support and a sense of community contribute to happiness.
  • Mental health and life expectancy are significant factors in well-being.
